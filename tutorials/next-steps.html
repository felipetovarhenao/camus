Next steps

If you’ve come this far, you’re more than ready to take full advantage of what GAMuT has to offer. However, it might be useful from time to time to check the Reference, to learn more about how GAMuT works, and what it can do.

Aside from that, everyone is welcome to:

  • Contribute to the development of GAMuT on Github.

  • Support the project with donations.

  • Report bugs you find through Github.

  • Share it with other people to help grow the community.
