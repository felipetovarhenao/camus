Next steps
============================

If you've come this far, you're more than ready to take full advantage of what **GAMuT** has to offer.
However, it might be useful from time to time to check the :doc:`../modules`, to learn more about
how **GAMuT** works, and what it can do.

Aside from that, everyone is welcome to:

* **Contribute** to the development of **GAMuT** on `Github <https://github.com/felipetovarhenao/gamut>`_.
* **Support** the project with `donations <https://paypal.me/felipetovarhenao>`_.
* **Report** bugs you find through `Github <https://github.com/felipetovarhenao/gamut>`_.
* **Share** it with other people to help grow the community.
